About us

We are AVALON ROV Team

We are enthusiastic and highly creative engineering students at the University of Sheffield, with a unique passion for UNDERWATER ROBOTICS. Our story began in 2017 when we became the first UK university team to participate in the international underwater robotics competition "MATE ROV" in the USA.
